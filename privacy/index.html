Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2, 2025
Last Updated: November 2, 2025

Verrons Labs (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the verrons.com website and related services. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

1. Data We Collect

We collect minimal personal data:

  • Contact requests: Email address and message content when you use our contact form
  • Server logs: IP address, browser type, pages visited (standard web server logs)
  • No tracking: We do NOT use analytics, cookies, or tracking scripts

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your contact inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect against fraud or abuse

We do not: Share, sell, or trade your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Retention

  • Contact inquiries: Retained for up to 1 year or until you request deletion
  • Server logs: Retained for up to 90 days, then deleted
  • Your rights: You can request deletion of your data at any time

4. GDPR Compliance

If you are in the EU, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to object: Opt-out of data processing
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, contact us →

5. Apple App Store Privacy Requirements

Verrons Labs apps and services:

  • Collect minimal data: Only what is necessary for functionality
  • No hidden tracking: No analytics, advertising networks, or third-party tracking
  • User consent: Request consent before collecting any personal data
  • Transparency: This privacy policy clearly discloses all data practices
  • Security: Data is transmitted securely and stored with appropriate safeguards

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • No storage of sensitive credentials on the website
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no security system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages and uses no third-party tracking, analytics, or marketing services. GitHub’s privacy policy applies to their hosting services; see GitHub Privacy Statement.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. We will notify you of material changes by updating the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: contact@verrons.com
Website: https://verrons.com

Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to transparency and protecting your data.
