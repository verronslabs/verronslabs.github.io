About Verrons Labs

We will see the future of technical documentation and cybersecurity—and we’re building it together.

Verrons Labs is founded by two passionate professionals dedicated to transforming technical communication and cybersecurity. We help teams build better documentation systems and secure their digital infrastructure.

Meet Our Founders

Dr. Valerie Renegar

Founder & Communication Strategist

Dr. Valerie Renegar holds the Herman Brown Chair and is Professor of Communication Studies at Southwestern University. She brings over a decade of experience in student development, leadership training, and organizational advancement. With a PhD in Communication Studies from the University of Kansas and an MA in Speech Communication from Kansas State University, Valerie combines academic excellence with practical expertise in human-centered communication.

Valerie has built a network of 500+ professional connections and is recognized for her innovative approaches to educational development. At Verrons Labs, she leads our communication and content strategy, ensuring our products and consulting services resonate with teams seeking clarity, accessibility, and impact.

Jeremy West

Founder & Security Lead

Jeremy West is Senior Cybersecurity Program Lead at the Center for Infrastructure Assurance and Security (UTSA) and brings extensive expertise in enterprise security and risk management. He holds the CISSP, CISM, and CEH certifications and earned his MS in Cybersecurity and Information Assurance from Western Governors University.

Jeremy has held key roles including Director of ISAO Standards and Director of Cybersecurity Professional Services at Avatier. He is an active member of ISACA and the International Information System Security Certification Consortium (ISC²), with over 370 professional connections. Jeremy's published work on cybersecurity process improvement for municipalities is recognized across the industry. At Verrons Labs, he leads our security and infrastructure strategy.
